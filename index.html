1EURO*

...is a Web Audio API installation. You are listening to the random drone soundscape of Konsti. All soundscapes are based on authentic field recordings.

1EURO is a minimalistic random audio pattern generator driven by howler.js. It plays a long background loop and simultaneously fires short samples in random order. Feel free to adopt and share this simple mechanism under GNU GPL-2.0-only.

*make sure your sound is on.
